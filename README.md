## 👩🏻‍🔬 About me

I am a **Bioengineer** and a **Master of Engineering with Honors**, and I will begin the **Doctoral Program in Science** at the **Universidad Autónoma de Baja California (UABC)** in August 2026.

My work lies at the intersection of **computing, artificial intelligence, bioengineering, and medicine**. I am interested in developing and evaluating computational methods for **medical image analysis**, with particular attention to their clinical relevance, reliability, and potential contribution to healthcare applications.

### 👁️ Master's thesis · 2024–2026

#### Automated Segmentation of Pathological Regions in Digital Fundus Images Using Artificial Intelligence

<div align="center">

![Digital Fundus Imaging](https://img.shields.io/badge/Digital%20Fundus%20Imaging-6A11CB?style=flat-square)
![Diabetic Retinopathy](https://img.shields.io/badge/Diabetic%20Retinopathy-7B2CBF?style=flat-square)
![Image Segmentation](https://img.shields.io/badge/Image%20Segmentation-4361EE?style=flat-square)
![Clinical Evaluation](https://img.shields.io/badge/Clinical%20Evaluation-4895EF?style=flat-square)

</div>

> Development and evaluation of artificial intelligence methods for identifying and segmenting pathological regions associated with **diabetic retinopathy** in digital fundus images.

The project combined computational and clinical evaluation to explore reliable tools for supporting the early identification and prioritization of patients with retinal abnormalities.

<details>
<summary><b>🔎 View methods and research components</b></summary>

<br>

- Digital image processing
- Hybrid convolutional and attention-based architectures
- U-Net and U-Net++ models
- Quantitative and qualitative evaluation
- Clinical assessment by specialists
- Interactive segmentation prototype

</details>
